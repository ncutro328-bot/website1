About Me

I am a graphic designer working in branding and typography and I approach my work with a minimalistic and intentional view. I see graphic design is both creative and methodical. In my design process, I begin by using my strong project planning and organizing capabilities to thoughtfully implement ideas, guidelines, and visual systems. When the opportunity arises, I also explore more personal themes, including family, culture, and nostalgia. My personal aesthetic is consistent and I apply it to projects by keeping things clean, simple, and appealing with a minimalist style when the project supports it. If it does not, I am flexible and can pivot my design choices to step outside of a minimalistic view to work to create something completely unexpected. My main goal as a designer is to create efficiency for viewers while maintaining appeal and interest.
